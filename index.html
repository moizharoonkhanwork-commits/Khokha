Our Story

Good food, hearty
portions, zero compromise.

Khans started with a simple idea: take the comfort food we grew up on and serve it the way our cities actually eat — fast, bold and unapologetically flavorful. It's a fusion of Pakistani and American kitchens, plated for the way you live now.

Cozy room, friendly faces and a menu built to share. Whether you're grabbing a quick paratha roll on the go or pulling up a table with the whole crew, you're family the moment you walk in.
